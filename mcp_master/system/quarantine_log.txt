# Mahoraga Quarantine Log
# Tracks quarantined files and regeneration attempts

## Quarantine Policy
- INFECTED: Files with executable threats (viruses, worms, trojans, ransomware, spyware, backdoors, rootkits, shellcode, obfuscated payloads, C2 patterns)
- CRITICAL: Required functionality that may contain exploits (moved to quarantine_critical for human review)
- RECOVERED: Safe, functionally equivalent versions regenerated by the system
